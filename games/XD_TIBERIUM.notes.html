Tiberian Dawn

Version 0.0.1


In 1995 a meteor struck down in Italy. From it a formerly unknown
type of crystal grew and quickly spread around the world. The new
substance was named Tiberium, with conflicting sources naming either
the river Tiber or the Roman Emperor Tiberius as its namesake.

Tiberium growth gathers valuable elements out of the ground and
concentrates them, making the crystals profitable to harvest.
An ancient cult claims the coming of Tiberium had been prohesized.
This organization, known as the Brotherhood of NOD, has secured
some of the richest sources of Tiberium and declared war on the
Global Defense Initiative (GDI), the multinational organization
tasked with securing world peace.

This is the war for control over Tiberium.

This is the Dawn of the Tiberian Age

Strategy
==========================================================


Strategical Hints for Beginners
  • The Forgotten can be player controlled, but recommendation is to have them AI controlled.
  • All GDI and NOD factions begin with a superweapon. Except for the Marked of Kane these are in their respective capitol territory.
  • All GDI and NOD factions begin with a Commando or Assassin special unit. Black Hand starts with three instead.
  • Some units like Mammoth and Stealth Tanks can only be built later in the game.
  • Double Hit units heal at the beginning of a player turn.
  • A Support Unit gives +1 Attack to one Supportable Unit.
  • Air Units need to end their turn in an area that was already controlled by a friendly faction at the beginning of the turn or on a Carrier.
  • Submarines attack first during naval battles unless countered by a Destroyer. Submarines cannot attack Air Units. They can submerge during combat.
  • To build a Construction Yard, an MCV must be present in that territory.
  • SAM-Sites and Construction Yards change owner when a territory is conquered.
  • All factions receive bonus units every turn if they own their capitol territory.
  • Enemy type units can be received in conquered enemy capitols.
  • NOD factions receive bonus income from some conquered territories.
  • Marked of Kane has to reconquer its capitol of Shantung from the Forgotten.
  • Marked of Kane has a chance to reenact the attack on Pearl Harbor
  • ZOCOM has the most spread out territories in Africa, South America, India, and Australia.
  • The Brotherhood of Nod is the most centralized power. Throwing GDI out of Europe is hard, but there are many high value territories. Spreading out into western Asia is also recommended to improve income.
  • The Central Command is situated around the North Atlantic. Shuttling troops from North America to Europe is vital for success.
  • The Steel Talons are GDI's power around most of the Pacific. Securing South America as well as contesting the eastern coasts of Asia is the path to victory.
  • Peace through Power.
